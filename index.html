How We Met

We were in the same coaching class and the same college back in 11th standard. It was in those Chemistry labs where we'd share laughs over the jokes Yash would tell, and those moments slowly paved the way for something special.

The Proposal

We were in Jaipur for a friend's wedding, and we had already planned a pre-wedding photoshoot. At the final venue, right in front of the majestic Hawa Mahal, instead of just posing, Yash proposed, and Rashmi, which we all already knew, said yes!

Our Future

Now we're excited to celebrate our love with all of our family and friends as we begin this new chapter of our lives together.
